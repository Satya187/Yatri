Adi Kailash, Uttarakhand

Adi Kailash, also known as Chhota Kailash, is a sacred mountain located in the Indian state of Uttarakhand. It is considered a holy pilgrimage site for devotees of Lord Shiva.

Kinnaur Kailash, Himachal Pradesh

Kinnaur Kailash is a mountain in the Kinnaur district of Himachal Pradesh, India. It is revered by both Hindu and Buddhist communities and is known for its breathtaking views and challenging trekking routes.

Srikhand Kailash, Himachal Pradesh

Srikhand Kailash is a mountain peak located in the Indian state of Himachal Pradesh. It is considered a sacred pilgrimage site for Hindus and is known for its challenging trekking trails and stunning natural beauty.

Chamba Kailash, Himachal Pradesh

Chamba Kailash is a mountain peak located in the Chamba district of Himachal Pradesh, India. It is revered by devotees and trekkers alike for its spiritual significance and stunning landscapes.

Chembra Peak, Kerala

Chembra Peak is the highest peak in the Wayanad district of Kerala, India. It offers breathtaking views of the surrounding hills and is a popular destination for trekking enthusiasts.

Deoria Tal and Chandrashila Trek, Uttarakhand

The Deoria Tal and Chandrashila Trek is a popular trekking route in Uttarakhand, India. It offers stunning views of the Himalayas and includes a visit to the beautiful Deoria Tal lake and the summit of Chandrashila Peak.

Goecha La Trek, Sikkim

The Goecha La Trek is a challenging trekking route in the Indian state of Sikkim. It offers panoramic views of the Himalayan peaks, including the majestic Kanchenjunga, the third highest mountain in the world.
