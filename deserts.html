Deserts

Welcome to the Deserts page. Discover the beauty and solitude of vast deserts.

Thar Desert, Rajasthan

The Thar Desert, also known as the Great Indian Desert, is a large arid region in the northwestern part of India and southeastern Pakistan. It is the world's 17th largest desert, and Rajasthan is the most prominent state with a major portion of the desert within its territory.

Rann of Kutch, Gujarat

The Rann of Kutch is a large salt marsh located in the Thar Desert in the Kutch district of Gujarat, India, and the Sindh province of Pakistan. It is famous for its white salt desert, the Great Rann of Kutch, which becomes an ethereal landscape during the Rann Utsav festival.

Spiti Valley, Himachal Pradesh

Spiti Valley, located in the Himalayas in the north-eastern part of Himachal Pradesh, is known for its stunning landscapes, high mountain passes, and Buddhist monasteries. It is often referred to as "Little Tibet" due to its resemblance to the Tibetan Plateau.

Great Rann of Cholistan, Rajasthan

The Great Rann of Cholistan is a vast desert located in the Thar Desert region of Rajasthan, India, and Punjab, Pakistan. It is characterized by its white salt flats and is renowned for the Rann Utsav, a cultural festival that celebrates the vibrant heritage of the region.
