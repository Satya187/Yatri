Beaches

Welcome to the Beaches page. Explore the beauty of beaches here.

Gokarna, Karnataka

Gokarna is known for its serene beaches, clear waters, and laid-back atmosphere. It's a popular destination for both relaxation and adventure.

Thottada & Bekal, Kerala

Thottada and Bekal are pristine beaches in Kerala known for their golden sands, clear waters, and stunning sunsets.

Havelock Island (Swaraj Dweep), Andaman & Nicobar Islands

Havelock Island, officially known as Swaraj Dweep, is famous for its white sandy beaches, crystal-clear waters, and vibrant coral reefs, making it a paradise for snorkeling and diving enthusiasts.

Varkala & Kovalam, Kerala

Varkala and Kovalam are famous for their cliff-top views, pristine beaches, and rejuvenating Ayurvedic treatments.
